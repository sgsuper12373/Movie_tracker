Your Name

Romance Fantacy Super natural ⭐ 8.4

Your Name is a Japanese animated film about two teenagers—Mitsuha, a girl from a quiet rural town, and Taki, a boy living in busy Tokyo—who mysteriously begin switching bodies without understanding why. As they experience life from each other’s perspective, they develop a meaningful connection and work together to understand the strange phenomenon linking them. Their story becomes a journey about fate, distance, and the invisible threads that connect people, even when they have never met.
